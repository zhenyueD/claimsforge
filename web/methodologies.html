📐 Auto-Synthesized Methodologies

The patterns ClaimsForge has distilled — without anyone writing them down. Every 5 resolved claims, the system clusters past cases and asks Gemini to write a reusable methodology. The methodology becomes a first-class KB entry that every future agent retrieves via hybrid-RAG.
Why this matters. Most "AI agent" platforms (e.g. Sierra, Decagon) require the merchant to author an SOP / AOP upfront — then compile it into an agent. ClaimsForge runs the loop in reverse: customers' real cases → auto-synthesized methodologies → KB → next agent. Zero SOP authoring.
